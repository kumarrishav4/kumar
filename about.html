About Hi, I'm Kumar Rishav

Architect of Digital Narratives and Multidimensional Innovator 

In the vast landscape of technology and creativity, I am Kumar Rishav, a spirited innovator and versatile developer with a fervent passion for turning ideas into tangible, awe-inspiring realities. With a full-stack proficiency encompassing web, game, and software development, I thrive on the dynamic challenges that come with crafting seamless digital experiences. My coding journey, spanning Python, Java, C#, and beyond, is not just about lines of code; it's a narrative unfolding in the digital realm, where every project is a testament to my commitment to pushing boundaries and exploring the uncharted. As a designer, my canvas extends beyond pixels and code, embracing the worlds of UI/UX design, 3D and 2D animation, and 3D CAD modeling. Each stroke and line is meticulously crafted to evoke emotions and deliver intuitive user experiences. Beyond the screen, I delve into the realm of creative technology, seamlessly blending hardware development, CAD design, and software robotics. My journey is a fusion of creativity and innovation, where I constantly seek new horizons to conquer. Embarking on this technological odyssey, I envision not just a career but a legacy of transformative ideas and groundbreaking solutions.

How I Got Here

My journey into the realm of technology and creativity began as a curious mind navigating the corridors of Holy Convent School and Brain International School. Fueled by a passion for problem-solving, I embarked on a coding odyssey, immersing myself in languages like Python and Java. This foundational knowledge paved the way for my pursuit of a Bachelor's degree in Electronics and Communication at Indraprastha Institute of Information Technology Delhi. Interning at HMI Lab, IIITD, provided a hands-on arena to apply my skills and kindled my interest in creative technology, spanning hardware development, CAD design, and robotics. Engaging actively on platforms like GitHub, LeetCode, and HackerRank, I've evolved into a versatile developer with proficiency in numerous programming languages, design tools, and electronic simulation software. Reflecting on this journey, each challenge has been a stepping stone, propelling me forward with an unwavering commitment to continuous learning and the boundless possibilities of technology.
